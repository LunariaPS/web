How to Play

Download the GTPS client for your device and open the game. Once you're in, you can create your own world, build anything you like, trade with others, and explore countless custom features. Whether you're here to chill, build, or compete the world is yours. Have fun and make it your own!

6 - Open Growtopia and connect!
